Managing Uncertainty

Vietnam is increasingly becoming a significant player in global supply chains. With the growth in Free Trade Agreements, foreign investments and industrial development, companies operating in Vietnam are plugging into international business networks and engaging in global sourcing and procurement for their production needs. Global supply chains, however, are vulnerable to disruptions caused by global economic, social and political events. Recent uncertainties brought about by events such as Brexit and the US elections have resulted in many companies retreating and re-thinking their strategies and operations. How should companies react amid these global developments to achieve growth? What is the real impact of these developments? How should companies modify their procurement and supply chain strategies to deal with a more unpredictable global environment?

Eastern International University (EIU) is pleased to present a seminar to provide insights and ideas on how best to manage procurement under current global difficulties and conditions. The seminar will also address the impact of global developments on procurement strategies and what high performing companies are doing to mitigate the effects of growing global uncertainty.

This seminar will feature experts in procurement from academia and industry to share their views and perspectives. The seminar format includes a keynote address followed by a panel discussion.
